Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aura Pic?

Aura Pic is a premium photo gallery website offering a wide range of high-quality wallpapers for mobile, desktop, and tablet devices. We provide both free and premium options to suit your needs.

How do I download wallpapers?

Browse our gallery, hover over an image, and click the "Download" button that appears. Free users get standard quality, while premium subscribers enjoy high-resolution downloads.

What are the benefits of a premium subscription?

Premium subscribers get access to high-quality images, a larger selection of photos, multi-device support, and priority help center access. Check our Pricing section for details.

Can I use these images commercially?

Our free wallpapers are for personal use only. Premium subscribers can contact us for licensing details if commercial use is required.

How do I contact support?

For any issues or inquiries, reach out via our Help Center (available to premium users) or email us at support@aurapic.com.
